API Integration

Farm Collective Wine are long-time Campaign Monitor customers who needed some help bridging the gap between their WineDirect CRM contacts and Campaign Monitor subscriber lists. That’s where Clearbold fits in.

Using the Slim PHP framework, Mark built a bridge app that periodically fetches four different Farm Collective brands’ contacts from WineDirect and imports them into subscriber lists via Campaign Monitor’s API to ensure that Farm Collective’s marketing emails are always reaching their full audience.

Onboarding and Customer Emails

Email is critical to SavingStar’s customer engagement and retention programs. The time had come to turbocharge their HTML email with consistent branding elements, more personality, and designs that work well on small device screens.

A planning and opportunity analysis workshop produced a comprehensive set of recommendations. With Aaron Taylor-Waldman running design, Clearbold delivered a series of coded, modular email layouts that can be mixed and matched in different combinations, integrated by the SavingStar team into their email service provider and proprietary platforms.
